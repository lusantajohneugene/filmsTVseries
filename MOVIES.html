House M.D.
Creator: David Shore
Stars: Hugh Laurie, Lisa Edelstein, Robert Sean Leonard
Dr. Gregory House is a brilliant yet highly antisocial medical genius who leads a team of diagnosticians at Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital. Known for his sharp sarcasm and unconventional methods, House solves rare and life-threatening medical cases that other doctors cannot figure out. Despite his extraordinary intelligence, he constantly struggles with chronic pain, addiction, and emotional isolation. His complicated relationships with his team and closest colleagues reveal the vulnerable side beneath his cold exterior. The series combines medical mysteries, psychological depth, and dark humor, making it both intellectually engaging and emotionally powerful.
